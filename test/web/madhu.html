Madhu Residency

Madhu Residency is situated in the heart of Kodaikanal and it is very nearer to the lake which is of walkable distance. This Le Castle under Dream Star Paradise is governed by an experts who are proficient in this field for 20 years. They are residing at Kodaikanal for more than 40 years and well known about the nook and corner of the town.

Romantic Duo Dwell

A perfect duo room for honeymoon or married couples to delightfully lavish the fondness between the couples. The duo dwell is not only to accommodate couples but also for two persons who planned to cherish the coolness of Kodaikanal. It is a compact room with double bed for a two member stay.

Quadratic Duplet Chamber

Double bed to accommodate 4 members to lavish the scenic nature and enjoy a serene vacation. It is specially designed for families to accommodate compositely. There is plenty of area for families to enjoy their individual space and also superfluous bed can be provided to lodge a surplus person. A small portico is available to enjoy with families and friends. This chamber is also suitable for gang of friends who could taste every flavors of Kodaikanal.

Nuclear Area

It is the most suited area for families who likes to delight the scenic beauty of Kodaikanal with clan and to reside with kinsfolk. Two separate bedrooms come under the nuclear area where you will have an individual space with your family. Alongside, a wide portico is available in the nuclear area to enjoy together with kinsfolk. Lodging at this area would be an official vacation space to have a separate area for your family alone and you will feel a home away from home at Kodaikanal.

Banquet Hall

A colloquium hall for seminars/ conference which can accommodate an aggregate of 20 to 30 persons.
