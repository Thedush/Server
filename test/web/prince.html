Prince Cottage

Prince Cottage is located at Vattakanal. Vattakanal is a hill station sort of village located 3km away from Kodaikanal, Tamil Nadu. One may find it an ideal place to retreat as the temperature is below 17 degree C during summer and is cold throughout the year. It is one of the best tourist places in recent times, where the foreigners never forget to visit such a splendorous village. Prince Cottage is located on such a serene environment which is very nearer to Dolphin’s Nose spot (important tourist spot). Trekking at early morning to Dolphin’s Nose would be a peculiar experience which will cherish in our memories for a prolonged time which is just few foot steps away from the cottage.

Cottage

It is an individual cottage which stands alone, which will provide you an adventurous vacation too. Very rarely and if we are fortunate, we could even expect to have a look at some of the wild animals from the cottage. Periyakulam view point is few yards from the cottage where we could see the top view of Periyakulam town. Fire camp is available in the cottage to cherish the customers. Also, modular kitchen is provided to prepare their own delicious food. Even we are privileged to prepare the mouth-watering food for the customers.
